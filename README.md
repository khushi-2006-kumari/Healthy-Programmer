Healthy Programmer:
It is a simple desktop reminder app designed to help programmers maintain a healthy routine while working long hours. It reminds you to drink water, take eye breaks, and do physical activity to stay healthy and productive.

Working hours: 9a.m. to 5 p.m.

Water Reminder:
-Plays water.mp3 to remind you to drink water.
-Target: 3.5 litres/day.
-Log each intake using the Drank log.

Eyes Exercise Reminder:
-Plays eyes.mp3 every 30 minutes to remind you to rest your eyes.
-Log completeion with Eyedone.

Physical Activity Reminder:
-Plays physixal.mp3 every 45 minutes to remind you to move/stretch.
-Log completion with Exdone.

Features:
-Audio reminders for water, eyes, and physical activity.
-Simple logging system to track progress.
-Runs in the background during working hours.

Benefits:
-Prevents dehydration.
-Reduce eye strain.
-Encourages regular physical activity to avoid long sitting health issues.
